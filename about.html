About Me

Hi guys! I'm Devanshi, a 17 year old from Mumbai with hopes to major in engineering and a PhD in procrastination.


My entire personality comes from the fact that I’ve taken Maths and Physics at a higher level, so you’ll probably hear me boast about that every now and then (nothing against the non science-math people, I’m sure having an actual life must be fun).
