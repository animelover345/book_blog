One Of Us Is Lying


A very interesting murder mystery.


Although the book was really good, it did not live up to the hype.


The book was a little slow slow-paced for my liking and seemed to drag on for quite a few chapters in the middle. But that’s probably the case with a lot of murder mystery books.


I fell in love with all the characters. The beautiful character development and well-written relationships were the highlights of this book.


For me, Addy’s character development was the best part of the book. Nate and Bronwyn’s relationship was beautifully written and they are currently one of my favorite YA couples.


The friendship that grew among the four Bayview students accused of murder was also well written. I hope to get more of these dynamics in the next book. Hands down an amazing read for anyone who loves a good murder mystery.
